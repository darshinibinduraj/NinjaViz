Visually Impaired

Trouble locating objects, it's distance, and name, particularly if they are not in their usual place or if they have been moved. Inability to distinguish between similar objects, particularly if they are not able to see the details that differentiate them. Difficulty assessing the distance of objects, which can make it hard to move around safely and independently.
